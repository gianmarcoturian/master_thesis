Here you receive an endowment of 100 points. You have to choose how much of this points to invest in a project and how much to keep.

The project might return 0.5 times the amount you invested (e.g. if you invest 50 and 0.5 realizes, you get 25 points) or 3 times the amount you invested. The relative likelihood of 3 is the same as 0.5 . Moreover, note that there might be various {{ if player.positive == True }} positive {{ endif }} unspecified return factors (they are displayed by a "??" in the table).

Assuming that you invest X points in option A, your payoff will be determined as follows:
X * return factor extracted from A + (100 - X) .
After your choice, a realization from Lottery A will be extracted and you will be paid accordingly. The results will be displayed at the end of the experiment.
